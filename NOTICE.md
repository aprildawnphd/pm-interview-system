# NOTICE

This repository contains a senior PM interview prep methodology system: artifacts, templates, discipline rules, and a worked example. It is published as an artifact of my non-coder AI-augmented building practice, alongside other projects in that portfolio.

---

## What this is

A methodology layer that pairs with an underlying AI coaching engine. The artifacts here are evergreen: a cross-company question bank, a leadership brand brief, a blank prep template, an SOP describing the workflow, discipline rules distilled from running the system across many loops, schema extensions to the underlying coaching state model, lessons learned from running the methodology through a 2026 job search, and one worked example.

The artifacts speak my voice. The methodology is structural. Both come from a real multi-loop senior PM job search rather than from an abstract framework.

---

## What this is NOT

- **Not a fork of the underlying coaching skill.** This repo is the methodology layer that consumes the skill, not a derivative of the skill code. See "Pairs with" below.
- **Not a maintained product.** Published as an artifact, not as a product accepting community contributions. There is no CONTRIBUTING.md, no issue templates, no roadmap.
- **Not a template kit.** The artifacts are voice-rich and method-specific. They illustrate how a senior PM might build and run a prep system, not a plug-and-play toolkit.
- **Not anonymized to the point of generic.** Source companies in my actual work history (Clarivate, ProQuest, Cengage, Everbridge, Deque) appear as my credentials because they are. Target companies from interviewed-for roles in 2026 are anonymized to exemplar descriptors because those are confidential interview contexts.

## Pairs with

[noamseg/interview-coach-skill](https://github.com/noamseg/interview-coach-skill) — Noam Segal's Claude Code-based interview coach, MIT-licensed. Noam's skill is the underlying coaching engine: it scores answers across 5 dimensions, runs decision-tree triage, manages a persistent `coaching_state.md`, and adapts to candidate-specific patterns. This repo is the methodology layer I built around the skill while running it through a multi-loop 2026 job search.

The relationship is composition, not derivation. None of Noam's code is included here. `coaching-state-extensions.md` documents the additions I made to the upstream coaching state schema while running the system; it cites Noam's upstream as the base.

If you are running Noam's skill: this repo offers methodology artifacts you can adapt to your own voice and stories, not a replacement skill.
